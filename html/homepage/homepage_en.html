Registration

To benefit from the services we are offering with HSGswisscovery, please create an account on our registration platform.

Attention! If you are affiliated with the University of St.Gallen (student, researcher, academia, staff) and are registering with a non-Swiss postal address: please link your edu-ID with your HSG account. Otherwise, you will not benefit from the full range of our services!

Search like a pro

HSGswisscovery offers three search profiles: «HSG» that is used as our default search as well as «swisscovery» and «swisscovery plus». What are the differences, what is included in your search?

HSG

  • Our physical holdings (books, journals and AV media)
  • Alexandria, the Institutional Repository of the University of St.Gallen
  • Our licensed electronic products (databases, E-journals and E-books)
  • Additional electronic content that is available Open Access

swisscovery

By selecting the search profile swisscovery, you can expand your search to all of Switzerland. Additionally to our content, you can find:

  • Physical holdings from all 500 libraries participating in our network
  • Additional free content that is made available by participating libraries in the network (Institutional Repositories, free E-collections and archives)

swisscovery plus

You can further expand your search scope with swisscovery plus. This will add the following to your results:

  • Physical holdings of libraries in the Renouvaud network (e.g. BCU Lausanne)
  • Bibliographical information for electronic documents without access
